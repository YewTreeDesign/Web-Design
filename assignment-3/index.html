Java Chip Muffins

Ingredients


  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 8 tablespoons (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 3 tablespoons instant espresso powder


Instructions

  1. Adjust an oven rack to the middle position and heat the oven to 375 degrees. Line a muffin tin with liners.
  2. Whish the flour, sugar, baking powder,baking soda and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk the milk and eggs together until smooth.
  4. Add the espresso powder and whisk until combined.
  5. Gently fold the buttermilk mixture into the flour mixture with a spatula until combined.
  6. Fold in the melted butter.
  7. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  8. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups.
  9. Bake until golden brown, 25-30 minutes, and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with only crumbs.
  10. Let cool in pan for 5 minutes before removing and placing on a wire rack to cool completely.
