Brief Intro

I've been drawing with pen on paper since I was four and I studied the fundamentals
while in school and college. Creating Art Digitally is a lot different from traditional media.

In my opinion, I prefer digital painting over traditonal Painting because it's more convenient but sometimes I like to break away to do traditonal media.

When I found out there are communities online that create art using software and tablets.At the time I stook with a mouse and created images on MS Paint because I didn't have the money to get that stuff because I was 11 at the time.

I then got a Wacom Bamboo Tablet. At First I found it uncomforable to use because I was use to looking down while I draw. After a while I got use to it. From there I continue to upgrade my hardware to increase the quality of my digital art and aswell ease of use.

This is all about what I've learned.

Software

The kind of art software I look for are ones that are able to read my table and it's, pressure sensitivity.

Another thing I look at is the variety of brushes if I want a pecticular texture. Aswell the ablility to change the resolution or dpi. It helps the quality of your digital art.

The programs I use

  • Photoshop
  • Procreate
  • Sai Paint Tool
  • Clip Studio Pro/Ex


Free programs I reccomend

  • Gimp


Hardware

Tablets can run expensive through Wacom, but they are really efficiant.
There are Cheaper alternatives to try out if you want to get comfortable with using a Tablets before you drop alot money on a wacom.
I reccommend Huion and XP-pen.
